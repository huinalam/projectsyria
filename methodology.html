WHERE DO WE COLLECT DATA?

UNHCR(United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees)

UNHCR provide various range of data about Syrian refugee. We collected the below list of data set from data.unhcr.org/syrianrefugees and popstats.unhcr.org
Data used:
A-1.Number of whole Syrian refugee per year from 1968 to 2014
A-2.Increased number of Syrian refugee per a month in middle east since 2012

World Bank

World Bank provides data set regarding economic indicators of each country. We collected GDP and GDP per capita of counturies in middle east to analysis migriant crisis by economic perspective.
Data used:
B-1:GDP and GDP per capita of middle east countries.

Syrian Tracker

syriatracker.crowdmap.com is an ushahidi based website which reports several damages inflicted against civilians in and around Syria. Several organizations and individual users can post report about what they witness during the war and which kind of damage they recieved. The website has huge amount of data of report sincec 2011. Among them, we specifically collect data about military action which damged to civil area included shelling, air strike, battle, chemical weapone and direct attack.
Data used:
C-1: Reports summary data per a day, from 2011 to 2016.

SHRN(Syrian Human Right NetWork)

SHRN is a human right organization in Syria. They also report important incidents happend during the civil war on their website and facebook. We also collected data of their report to cover vacancy of specific period in Syrian tracker's data

Violation Documentaion Center in Syria

They collect detail data of casualties during the civil war. They have collected and suumarized since 2011.
Data used:
1.The number of death per a month since 2011.

We used data by following step
1.Translating C-1 data
2.Extracted important keywords by frequency of occurrence. These keywords indicate type of attacks and group who might be involved in the attacks
3.Cleansing C-1 data by seperating huge cluster of text into meaningful senteces or paragraphs which indicaite a single event. 4.Counting how many specific type of event happned per a month by keywords.
